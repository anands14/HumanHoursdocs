Common questions

The app shows no data.

Open Human Hours and grant Screen Time access when prompted (or enable it under iOS Settings › Screen Time). Your usage fills in from your own device.

App Limits aren’t blocking apps.

App Limits is a Human Hours Pro feature. Make sure Pro is active and that you’ve granted Screen Time access, then set a daily limit on an app.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Settings inside the app › tap the Pro option › Restore.

How is my data handled?

Your Screen Time and journal data stay on your device and are never sent to us. See our Privacy Policy.
