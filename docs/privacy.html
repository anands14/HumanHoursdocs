Human Hours — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Human Hours is designed to be private by default.

Screen Time data

Human Hours uses Apple’s Screen Time (Family Controls / Device Activity) APIs to show your app usage, pickups, and to power app limits. This data is processed and stored only on your device, inside the app’s secure App Group container. It is never transmitted to us or to anyone else, and we never see it.

No account

Human Hours has no login and no user account. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, or any advertising identifier.

Your journal entries

Unlock Journal entries you create are stored locally on your device only.

Subscriptions

Human Hours Pro is sold through Apple In-App Purchase and managed with RevenueCat. To validate your subscription and prevent fraud, RevenueCat processes your purchase/transaction data (Purchase History). This is not linked to your identity (no name, email, or IP address is collected) and is never used to track you across apps or for advertising. See revenuecat.com/privacy.

No tracking or ads

We do not track you, show ads, or share data with data brokers or advertising networks.
